Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: What is mental health support?

Answer: Mental health support refers to services and resources that help individuals manage their mental health and emotional well-being.

Question 2: How can I seek help?

Answer: You can seek help by contacting a mental health professional or reaching out to support groups.

Question 3: What resources are available?

Answer: There are various resources available, including hotlines, therapy, and online support groups.
